Minimum System Requirements:

  • Windows 7/Windows 8.1/Windows 10 (64 bit OS).
  • Intel i3 or better multicore processor, 2GHz or above. (Intel 6th Gen or newer CPU recommended for HD and 4K Videos).
  • 4 GB RAM (8GB required for HD and 4K videos).
  • ntel HD Graphics 5000 or later; NVIDIA GeForce GTX 700 or later; AMD Radeon R5 or later. 2 GB vRAM (4GB required for HD and 4K videos).
  • At least 10GB free hard-disk space for installation (SSD-Solid State Disk recommended for editing HD and 4K videos)
  • Internet connection is necessary for software registration and access to online services like Effects Store.
